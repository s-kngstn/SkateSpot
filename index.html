The What

SkateSpot is a free to use application that locates peer-selected & peer-reviewed skateboarding locations near you.

The How

Users who register to the website can post a photo, location, and review any location they found and enjoyed skating.


Locations will be shared publicly so other skaters can skate the same SkateSpot.


Rate, check-in, and keep eveyone up to date with SkateSpots in your neighborhood.
